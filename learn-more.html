Plocket is a small business set up by the young enterprise group at Reed's School.

We have created a charging solution for your phone that allows it to hang from the wall socket so you charging cable does not get in the way of a path, or if there is no table available to put your phone down to charge. We aim to make plockets sustainably - all plockets are made from recycled clothing, each one is unique.
